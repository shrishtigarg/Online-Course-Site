F.A.Q's

Where can I join the CIBOP program?

You can opt for our CIBOP program either online or in-class at any of our centres in Mumbai, Thane, Pune, Bangalore, Delhi, Chennai, Coimbatore, Cochin, Hyderabad and Kolkata.

How can I join?

You can join our flagship CIBOP program online or classroom at any of the centres in Mumbai, Thane, Pune, Bangalore, Delhi, Chennai, Coimbatore, Cochin, Hyderabad and Kolkata.

What is the format of the program?

The CIBOP program is a 180-hour classroom or online training program that provides aspirants with an in-depth understanding of products, processes and operations and prepares them to take on roles in investment banking.

What is the duration of the program?

The CIBOP is a 2-month program done full-time (on weekdays), and a 3 month program if done part-time on the weekends. Please contact the nearest centre for more information.

What is the eligibility criteria for placements?

In order to be eligible for placement, candidates need to clear all Imarticus exams with at least 60% grades and ensure an average attendance of at least 80% in each of the three levels.

What are the fees for the program?

The investment banking course costs INR 1,30,000 /-. You can pay by credit card, debit card or net banking from all leading banks. We offer EMI loan options as well.
